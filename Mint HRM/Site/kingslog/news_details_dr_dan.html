Dr. Dan leaves for visiting lecturing at Dalian Maritime University

Leading international academic on Law of the Sea and Maritime Law who is presently the Executive Director of Ceylon Shipping Corporation has left the island of Sri Lanka to deliver series of lectures on 'Contemporary issues in the Use of the Seas within Public and Private Law Perspectives" at Dalian Maritime University (DMU), China. During the two weeks' stay at DMU, he is expected to outline some important areas in Law of the Sea and Maritime Law taking several case studies of the Sri Lankan context. Dr. Dan is also the Consultant Dean of CINEC Maritime Campus and Maritime Consultant of KINGSLOG
