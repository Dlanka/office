Speaker

Ruki De Silva

Currently he is the Chairman & CEO of 361 Degree Holdings Ltd. which operates two training brands; 361 Degrees and Wild Drift. The two entities together form are arguably the largest corporate consulting and training company in Sri Lanka, delivering 273 days of training and consultancy in the year 2015 alone.

He and his team are capable of delivering interventions in the areas of:

  • Corporate/ Business/ Strategic Planning from crafting a business model canvas to articulating individual KPI score cards.
  • Organizational alignment, strengthening, change management and restructuring
  • Capability development through training/development, interventions in strategic management, general management, soft skills and selected hard skills.
  • Employee engagement through interactive communication campaigns to promote and strengthen elements of brands, cultures and new practices as well as team building.
  • Leadership development at all levels through training, coaching, project deployment and stretch assignments.

All these interventions are delivered with an 'innovative touch' combining best in class business theories with novel experiences topped with an infusion of modern day technology, blending a balanced training environment.

Though he earned a Post Graduate Marketing Diploma form CIM, UK he pursued his Masters studies in Business Psychology at the Heriott Watts University in UK. Currently he is reading for a PhD with a research interest in the effect of psychological biases in teams. He is also a Certified National Trainer of Junior Chamber International Training Institute, USA.
