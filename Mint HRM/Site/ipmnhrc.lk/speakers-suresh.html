Speaker

Dr. Suresh Marcandan

Dr. Suresh Marcandan, a Sri Lankan born Australian, is an entrepreneur, management consultant and development strategist. He is currently the Managing Director of a multi-million dollar investment holding company – Sugirtham Holdings Ltd., which has a diversified portfolio of strategic investments in property development, real estate, hospitality and tourism, shipping, oil and gas engineering, executive education, marine consulting and management consulting across Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore, South Africa, Nigeria and Sri Lanka.

Dr. Suresh is also the Managing Director of People Power International Sdn. Bhd., a boutique business advisory and management consulting practice specialising in delivering customised, value-added total consulting solutions in the areas of business strategy and sustainable competitive advantage, strategic marketing management, strategic human resource management, change management and leadership development. He has provided consulting solutions to multinational and local companies in the United Kingdom, Nigeria, Dubai, India, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore, The Philippines, and Australia. Prior to venturing into full-time management consulting, he served as a CEO of reputed shipping companies in Asia.

Dr. Suresh was also employed as an international expert in ship operation and ship management by a public-listed Singaporean shipping company in the period 1993 to 1996, during which time he was also appointed as a Consultant in Shipping and had the honour of representing the Government of Singapore at an UN specialist agency - the International Maritime Organisation, London. He is a great admirer of the Singaporean system of good governance and in particular its visionary founding principles of meritocracy, multiculturalism and secularism.

His academic interests include strategic management and managing corporate reputation, both of which he teaches on the PG Diploma in Marketing course offered by the Chartered Institute of Marketing (UK) and the MBA offered by the University of Strathclyde. He is a strong advocate of the "Systems Thinking" school of thought, and his doctoral thesis in 1999 was titled "Translating Strategy into Action – A Total Systems Intervention Approach".

Dr. Suresh holds the following post-graduate degrees - Doctor of Business Administration (UK), Master of Business Administration (UK), Master of Laws (UK), Professional Graduate Diploma In Marketing (UK) and Graduate Certificate of Education (Australia). He is an alumnus of the Harvard Kennedy School of Government (USA) and the Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy (Singapore)
