Retirement Redefined

Retiring from one’s job is a very significant and dramatic event. If well planned, it can be the beginning of a glorious period ahead. If not, it may turn into a frustrating and disillusioning one.

Today’s retirees are far from being “over-the-hill.” Armed with life’s experiences, accumulated work skills and well-weathered wisdom, this phase of their lives may yet be their most productive and satisfying.

This short talk highlights some important issues related to retirement.
