Be Your Own Boss

The Entrepreneurial Revolution: It's time to be...YOUR OWN BOSS

Looking for a job...fed-up with your current job...fed-up of your working environment...looking for something more challenging and fulfilling...think you are worth more than what you are paid...looking for a second source of income ... retired but restless ... want to keep busy and earn some money at the same time ... want to turn your hobby into a business?

Good. Attend the "It's time to ... Be Your Own Boss Talk Series". It could be the best investment of your time ever.

Some talk highlights ...

  • Clear some misconceptions about going into business.
  • Learn the truths behind the many business myths.
  • Open your mind to some not normally thought of business ideas and opportunities.
  • Get some tips on choosing a business to get into and starting your business right to enhance the chances of its success.

And, of course, make some new friends who may be your future business partners / associates / customers.

  • Session 2 – Looking For A Business
    • Looking for business ideas
    • From ideas to opportunities
    • A product looking for a market
    • A market looking for a product
    • Looking for a business – some other approaches
    • Readymade businesses
  • Session 3 - Getting Started – Doing It Right
    • Name and business structure

    • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
    • Looking for a suitable location for the business
    • Making sure I have the money
    • Some Government funding and support schemes
  • Session 4 – Reaching My Customers
    • Knowing exactly what I am selling
    • Knowing who my customers are and what they are looking for when they buy
    • Making the 4 Ps work
    • Reaching my customers
    • Connecting with customers online
  • Session 5 – Money Matters
    • Knowing the financing needs of my business – minimising the financing needs
    • Importance of keeping financial records

  • Session 6 – Growing My Business
    • Do I really want to grow my business ?
    • Growing my business - knowing the challenges that I will face
    • Some models / approaches – pros and cons
    • Ready to cash out ?
