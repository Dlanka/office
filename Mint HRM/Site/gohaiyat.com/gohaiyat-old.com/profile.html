Goh Ai Yat, a former senior banker, is the managing consultant of SBF Group of consulting companies.

Entrepreneur, Educator, Trainer/Coach, Storyteller, Artist

Ai Yat advocates for agility at work and in business to succeed in an ever-changing environment. She challenges students, professionals, and entrepreneurs to be highly marketable and business driven, and make decisions that are not only technically correct, but also make good business sense. She regularly conducts entrepreneurial and development programmes. She trains organisational employees to adopt entrepreneurial thinking in preparation for increasingly competitive business landscapes, and individuals who are aspiring entrepreneurs. She also helps retirees to be active and make use of their expertise in their golden years.

Academic Programmes

Ai Yat teaches Entrepreneurship for the RMIT University degree programme at SIM Global Education and Entrepreneurship and Strategic Finance Issues for an Australian MBA program. She coaches and mentors undergraduates and professionals-in-transit to embark on their career of choice or starting a small business.

Business Skills

Ai Yat trains and equip people with business skills to help them stay relevant in today’s challenging operating environment. Her programmes include:

  • The Back of the NapkinTM - Solving Problems with Pictures
  • Starting Your Own Business, The Self-Employed Training Workshops
  • The Entrepreneurial Manager, The Entrepreneurial Employee
  • Thriving On Challenges
  • Visual Thinking, Creativity & Innovation - The Rules Have Changed!
  • Networking & Relationship Management
